About me

My name is Sébastien Lozano-Forero, Professor, Mathematician, Statistician and researcher, currently working as Data Analyst and professor in the higher education market.

As of today, I work as Data Analyst at the Lambton College, located in beautiful South Western Ontario (Canada), and as Remote Professor at Fundación Universitaria Los Libertadores (Colombia). At the college, my job consists on delivering better ways to present information to stakeholders, faculty and managers and thus, contributing to the decision making process and overall student well being, by means of data products deployment.

As my passion for education (and learning) only grows with time, I am deeply interested in learn about how other industries works while being able to provide value to it through data related products.
